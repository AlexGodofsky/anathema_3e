Core Rules
==============
Melee
*Orichalcum: +1 Acc, +1 Def, +1 Speed
*Moonsilver: +2 Acc
*Jade: +3 Speed
*Starmetal:+2 Damage
*Soulsteel: +1 Accuracy (and drains motels equal to wielders permanent essence)

Ranged
*Orichalcum: +1 Acc, +1 Damage, +50 Range for bows
*Moonsilver: +1 Acc, +100 Range for bows 
*Jade: +1 Rate, +50 Range for bows
*Starmetal: +2 Damage
*Soulsteel: +2 Accuracy (but only when used against living targets)

Armour
*Orichalcum: +2 Soak L/B
*Moonsilver: No mobility penalty
*Jade: No fatigue value
*Starmetal: Reduces attackers damage roll success by 1
*Soulsteel: +2 Soak L/B


Power Combat
==============
Wie Core Rules, lediglich Orichalcum/Melee gibt einen Rate-Bonus statt dem Speed-Bonus

Melee
*Orichalcum: +1 Acc, +1 Def, +1 Rate

Second Edition
==============
Melee
*Orichalcum: +1 Acc, +1 Def, +1 Rate
*Moonsilver: +2 Acc, +2 Def
*Jade: -1 Speed, +1 Damage
*Starmetal: +1 Acc, +3 Damage
*Soulsteel: +2 Accuracy (and drains motels equal to wielders permanent essence)

Ranged
*Orichalcum: +1 Acc, +1 Damage, +50 Range for bows / +10 Range for thrown
*Moonsilver: +1 Acc, +100 Range for bows / +20 Range for thrown
*Jade: -1 Speed, +50 Range for bows / +10 Range for thrown
*Starmetal: +1 Acc, +2 Damage
*Soulsteel: +2 Accuracy, +2 Damage (but only when used against living targets)

Armour
*Orichalcum: +2 Soak L/B, +1 Hardness L/B
*Moonsilver: No mobility penalty
*Jade: No fatigue value
*Starmetal: +1 Hardness L/B, reduces attackers damage rolls by a -1 external penalty
*Soulsteel: +2 Soak L/B, +1 Hardness L/B
