An Abyssal with Essence below or equals to three may have any appearance he or she wishes to have.

Starting Abyssals have Essence two. Testing extremes is more interesting, so we'll look at three.

Every value of Appearance should be allowed - since later tests will forbid the middle range, we test indeed have to test all values.
